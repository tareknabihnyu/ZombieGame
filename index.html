Forest Zombie Invasion

How to Play

Objective: Survive waves of zombies in a forest setting, using strategic movement and combat skills to stay alive.

Controls:

  • Movement: Use [WASD] or arrow keys to move around.
  • Shoot: Click the left mouse button to shoot at zombies.

Gameplay Tips:

  • Stay on the Move: Constant movement makes it harder for zombies to catch you.
  • Use the Environment: Hide behind trees and use the terrain to your advantage.
  • Watch for Different Zombies: Some may be faster or tougher. Adjust your strategy accordingly.

Progression: With each wave, zombies become tougher. Survive as long as you can and beat your high score!
